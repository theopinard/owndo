Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

📋

Tasks & Projects

Organize tasks in your Inbox or group them into projects. Add descriptions, subtasks, and manage everything in one place.

☑️

Subtasks

Break down complex tasks into subtasks. Check them off inline from the task list without leaving the screen.

📅

Deadlines & Reminders

Set a deadline and get a notification reminder. Deadlines show as human-friendly labels — "Today", "Tomorrow", "3 days ago".

☁️

Dropbox Sync

Your data syncs to your own Dropbox account. Last-write-wins merge keeps everything consistent across devices.

✈️

Works Offline

All data lives locally in SQLite. Create and complete tasks with no connection — changes sync automatically when you're back online.

🔒

Private by Default

No analytics, no telemetry, no accounts. Your tasks go straight to your Dropbox and nowhere else.

How it works

Simple setup, instant sync.

Download the app

Install owndo on Android, Linux, or macOS from the GitHub releases page.

Connect Dropbox

Sign in with your Dropbox account. owndo stores only your access token — your password never leaves Dropbox.

Start capturing tasks

Add tasks to your Inbox or any project. Changes sync automatically in the background every 45 seconds.

Access anywhere

Install on another device and sign in with the same Dropbox account — your tasks appear instantly.
