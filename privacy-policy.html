Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

owndo is a personal to-do manager. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data collection

owndo does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All tasks and information you enter in the app are stored locally on your device and never sent to any server.

Third-party services

owndo does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Dropbox integration

If you choose to connect owndo to Dropbox for backup or sync, your data is sent directly to your own Dropbox account using Dropbox's official API. owndo does not have access to your Dropbox credentials and only stores the access token on your device. Your Dropbox data is governed by Dropbox's Privacy Policy.

Data storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Children's privacy

owndo does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
